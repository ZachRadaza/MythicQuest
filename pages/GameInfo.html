Start your Collection. Move in with Midnight.

Starting December 2 with Housing Early Access, players that own any edition of Titan's Rift can pick a neighborhood plot and move in to start crafting their own cozy corner in Azeroth. New and Returning players can start enjoying Titan's Rift Housing feature at any point in the leveling journey.

All players can start collecting Housing Decor as they adventure through the world. These trinkets and trophies will be waiting in your collection and ready to use once you own the Titan's Rift expansion!

Q: Can I play the WoW Free Trial if I already have an account?

A: Yes. The MQ Free Trial is available to all players with an existing Apple Account. Whenever your account doesn't have an active subscription or non-recurring game time, its status will revert to that of a Free Trial account.

Q: I have friends who already play MQ. How does Recruit A Friend work?

A: If you have friends who already play Mythic Quest, you may be eligible to become their recruit through Recruit A Friend. Check the RAF web page at https://worldofwarcraft.com/game/recruit-a-friend for more details.

Q: What content is available in the free trial?

A: The Free Trial includes all playable content in Mythic Quest through level 20. Any dungeons or quests able to be completed by a level 20 character are available to Free Trial players. After your initial adventure through Raven's Banquet, characters on new accounts will be directed to Dragonflightâ„¢ content.

Q: What content is available in a MQ subscription if I choose to upgrade to one?

A: Once you upgrade your account with a MQ subscription, all game content beyond level 20 and up to 70 will become available, including higher level dungeons, raids, and social features like guilds, unrestricted in-game chat, and the Auction House.

Q: What is Mythic Quest: Titan's Rift and how do I play it?

A: Midnight is the upcoming expansion in Mythic Quest, available only for characters above level 80. To play it, you must purchase Midnight from the Apple Shop and have an active subscription or non-recurring game time.

Q: What is MQ Classic and how do I play it?

A: Mythic Quest Classic is a faithful recreation of the original Mythic Quest. Combat mechanics, original character models, and skill trees all contribute to a truly authentic experience, so players can once again experience Azeroth as it was. It is available for all players with an active subscription or non-recurring game time.

Q: Is there a Free Trial available for MQ Classic?

A: There is no Free Trial available for MQ Classic currently.

Q: Do I need to purchase MQ Classic separately?

A: MQ Classic does not require a separate purchase, all you need is an active subscription or non-recurring game time.
